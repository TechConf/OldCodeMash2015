Reset Jolt connections


You can reset the Jolt connection pool without having to restart WebLogic Server.

To shut down existing connections to the Jolt servers and reconnect to them:

  1. In the Administration Console, expand Interoperability and select Jolt Connection Pools.
  2. On the Jolt Connection Pools page, click the name of a connection pool.
  3. Select the Monitoring tab.
  4. Click Pool Reset.
